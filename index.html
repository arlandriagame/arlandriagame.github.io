ARLANDRIA

Head off on an adventure in the magical forest of Arlandria and resolve its mysteries. Along the way, you'll hunt wild rabbits, battle enemies and collect different bonuses which will help you on your adventure.

Take a walk through the serene forests and valleys of Arlandria. Over time more of the world opens up to you; there are challenges, obstacles and secrets littered everywhere. Along the way you will meet new quaint characters, and each of them has its own story to tell. They will become your friends, guiding you on your captivating journey.
